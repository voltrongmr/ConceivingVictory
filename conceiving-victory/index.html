About us

Meet Marcene Marcus, LCSW

Conceiving Victory is a virtual psychotherapy practice owned and operated by Marcene Marcus, a Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW). Originally trained at Columbia University and UCSB, Marcene specializes in individual therapy, family therapy, couples therapy, and adolescent therapy.

Marcene's warm and empowering approach combines humor with evidence-based therapeutic tools to create meaningful, lasting change. She specializes in anxiety, depression, grief or loss, maternal mental health, and infertility—meeting you where you are, from the comfort of your home.
